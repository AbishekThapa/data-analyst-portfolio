About Me

I am Abishek Thapa, an aspiring data analyst passionate about transforming raw data into clear, actionable insights. Currently, I am a student at Torrens University in Brisbane, pursuing my Master's in Business Information Systems.

With hands-on detailed project in SQL, Python, Power BI, and ETL pipelines, I have built and optimized databases, designed interactive dashboards, and delivered meaningful visualizations that tell compelling stories. My projects include developing a South East Queensland Bus Route Database using GTFS data, creating an Australian Housing Market Power BI dashboard with advanced DAX, and engineering an ETL process for the Yelp Academic Dataset using PostgreSQL and Jupyter Notebook.

I thrive at the intersection of problem-solving and data storytelling, with a strong focus on clean, efficient, and performance-optimized solutions. I am eager to contribute my analytical expertise to solve real-world business challenges and support data-driven decision-making.
